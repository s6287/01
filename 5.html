Persona 2: John - The Student

Age: 23

Occupation: University Student

Pain Points: Limited budget, often distracted by social media.

Goals: Save money and stay organized.

Devices Used: Mobile, Laptop

Empathy Map: John

Says

"I need something that fits my student budget." - "I get distracted too often."

Thinks

"How can I make the most out of my limited resources?" - "I need a solution to stay productive."

Does

Uses free tools and consumes content during downtime. Looks for discounts and student-friendly features.

Feels

Stressed about balancing studies, part-time job, and social life. Motivated by affordability and productivity.

Persona 3: Emily - The Entrepreneur

Age: 35

Occupation: Founder of an Online Business

Pain Points: Needs automation tools, frustrated by lack of integration.

Goals: Focus on business growth and automate tasks.

Devices Used: Desktop, Mobile

Empathy Map: Emily

Says

"I need tools that help automate tasks." - "I want seamless integration across platforms."

Thinks

"How can I focus on growing my business, not menial tasks?" - "I need tools that scale with my business."

Does

Uses automation tools, manages social media scheduling, and business operations through mobile and desktop.

Feels

Excited about business growth, but frustrated with manual tasks. Seeks efficiency and automation.
